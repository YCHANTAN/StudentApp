# ENROLLMENT_RULES.md

## Tuition
- Cost per unit: 1500

## Fixed Fees
- Registration Fee: 1000
- Library Fee: 500
- Technology Fee: 800

## Calculation
Total Tuition = (Units × Cost per unit) 
               + Registration Fee 
               + Library Fee 
               + Technology Fee

## Required Student Fields
- Full Legal Name: required, min 3 chars
- Student ID Number: required, format SXXXXX
- Email Address: required, valid email
- Phone Number: required, 10-12 digits
- Contact Person Name: required
- Relationship: required
- Emergency Phone Number: required, 10-12 digits

## Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit Card: Enabled
- Online Banking: Enabled
- Cash at Counter: Enabled

## Receipt Requirements
- Student Information
- Course + Units
- Tuition Breakdown
- Total Amount Paid
- Payment Method
- Timestamp
